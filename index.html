People Sourcing Crew

People Sourcing Crew is a sourcing and recruitment company that solves your recruitment needs.

We work all over the world, we’re brilliant at reaching people, and we work with everyone,
from zealous niche start-ups to multinational corporates.

HOW WE DO IT

People Sourcing Crew looks after you short-term and long-term.

We offer a number of recruitment packages, each one completely customisable.

We also provide recruitment consultancy and training.

This is about how you attract the right people, so that you can become the sort of company you want to be.

It is about developing you as an employer brand.

It’s quite simple actually, we just use magic. But hey, luckily we can teach you too.

RECRUITMENT

SOURCING
&
TALENT MAPPING

We source a list of at least 30 candidates for the vacancy required.

This list will contain all the information you need for you to get in touch with those candidates one-on-one: phone number, e-mail, as well as Twitter, Facebook and Linked In accounts.

SHORT RECRUITMENT
PROCESS

We provide you with an in-house recruiter for at least 1 day a week for 6 weeks.

They team up with your hiring manager, create a list of the best candidates, and get people in for interviews.

FULL RECRUITMENT
PROCESS

This is for when you need to fill multiple vacancies.

We provide you with an in-house recruiter (part or full time,
whatever you require) and they do the whole end-to end process, right through to final hirings.

FREELANCE

We provide you with a freelancer.

We have seasoned IT and sales recruiters spread all over the world, our network ensuring there is always someone available to work for you.

CONSULTANCY
& TRAINING

In this digital age you can go from a three-person start-up to employing over 100 people in the space of a year.

Established organisations also expand at a surprising rate. So you need strategies, processes and structures in place to cope with the recruitment demands.

We provide training and advice on selling yourself through the jobs you offer.

This can mean taking a long hard look at what your company stands for, the culture you wish to establish, and what you want to achieve.

But since it means attracting the best recruits, the results are infinitely worth it.

Our service covers three main areas:

RECRUITMENT
TRAINING

Together, we look at recruitment channels and the best way to use them.

We show you how to target your recruitment advertising and source from as wide a pool as possible.

Advanced sourcing is also covered – how to find candidates with specific abilities for specific roles, and finding and sourcing from different databases

CONTENT, MESSAGING
AND REACH

We help you define the personalities of the individuals you are looking for,

and show you how to build systems to find them. We look at how you present yourself online,

and at writing recruitment ads that attract the right people and build your company brand.

PROCESSES

Without quality recruitment processes the best people will go and work for your competitors.

Through workshops and training, we help you set up a complete recruitment engine, one that dovetails with your vision for your company.
